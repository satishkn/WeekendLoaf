recipe:
  servings: 1 loaf
  prep: 18 hours (with overnight proof)
  cook: 35 minutes
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 500g bread flour
    * 325ml water
    * 10g salt
    * 5g instant yeast
    * 1 tablespoon cumin seeds, toasted
    * 1 tablespoon coriander seeds, toasted
    * 1 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
    * 2 green chillies, minced
    * 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
    * 1 tablespoon fresh coriander, chopped
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Toast the spices in a dry pan for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
    2. Mix flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl.
    3. Add water gradually and mix until shaggy dough forms.
    4. Fold in toasted spices, chilies, ginger, and fresh coriander over 4-5 sets (autolyse for 30 mins first).
    5. Let dough rest for 30 minutes, then perform a series of stretches and folds every 30 minutes for 2.5 hours.
    6. Shape and place in banneton or bowl lined with cloth.
    7. Refrigerate overnight (12-16 hours).
    8. Pre-heat Dutch oven to 500°F for 45 minutes.
    9. Score the loaf and bake covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered for 15-18 minutes until deep golden brown.
    10. Cool on wire rack for at least 1 hour before slicing.
---
