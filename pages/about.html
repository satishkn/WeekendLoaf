Weekend Loaf was born from a love for bold spices and slow baking. We believe bread should taste like something — warm cumin, toasted coriander, fresh herbs — not like a shelf.

Every loaf is mixed by hand, proofed overnight, and baked in small batches so you get something truly fresh. We bake on weekends, which means each order is made with the kind of care that only comes when you're not rushing.
