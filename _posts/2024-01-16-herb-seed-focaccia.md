title: Herb & Seed Focaccia
categories:
  - Breads
featured_image: /images/hero.jpg
recipe:
  servings: 1 loaf
  prep: 16 hours
  cook: 25 minutes
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 450g bread flour
    * 300ml water
    * 8g salt
    * 5g instant yeast
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
    * 1 tablespoon nigella seeds (kalonji)
    * 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
    * Sea salt for topping
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Mix flour, salt, and yeast with water.
    2. Add olive oil during hydration phase.
    3. Stretch and fold over 2.5 hours.
    4. Let rise overnight in refrigerator.
    5. Shape into a half-sheet by gently stretching dough and dimpling with wet fingers.
    6. Top with sesame, nigella seeds, rosemary, and sea salt.
    7. Proof for 30 minutes.
    8. Bake at 475°F for 20-25 minutes until golden.
    9. Cool on rack and drizzle with good olive oil.
---
