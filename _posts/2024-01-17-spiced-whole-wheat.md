  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 300g bread flour
    * 200g whole wheat flour
    * 325ml water
    * 10g salt
    * 5g instant yeast
    * 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
    * 1 tablespoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
    * 1 teaspoon black mustard seeds
    * 1 tablespoon jaggery or honey
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Toast all seeds in a dry pan until fragrant (2-3 minutes).
    2. Mix flours, salt, yeast, and jaggery.
    3. Add water gradually and incorporate toasted seeds.
    4. Stretch and fold every 30 minutes for 3 hours.
    5. Shape and cold proof overnight (12-16 hours).
    6. Pre-heat Dutch oven to 500°F.
    7. Score deeply and bake covered for 25 minutes.
    8. Uncover and bake for 15-18 minutes until deep brown.
    9. Rest completely before slicing (at least 1 hour).
